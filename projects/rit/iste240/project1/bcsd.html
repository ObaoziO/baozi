Brighton Central Schools

Brighton has four public schools: Council Rock Primary School (CRPS), French Road Elementary School (FRES), Twelve Corners Middle School (TCMS), and Brighton High School (BHS). I have attend all but CRPS in Brighton, and both of siblings started primary school here. I enjoy being in the Brighton environment as there are a lot of diversity, especially cultural and religious diversity.
