Malls

Source: Wikimedia Commons

Of all the malls in Rochester, Marketplace Mall is one of the two malls I visit most frequently. Eastview Mall would be the other one. Compare to the other malls, both malls are within 15 minutes car ride from where I live. Not only that, Marketplace is five to seven minutes closer than Eastview.

Why would I go to Eastview if Marketplace is closer? There are more stores available and the whole Eastview Mall itself is comparably much larger than Marketplace.
