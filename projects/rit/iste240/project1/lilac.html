Lilac Festival

Photographer: Yuqing Guo

Rochester Lilac Festival is an annual event that runs for about a week during May, around Mother's day. It's a big event so roads are blocked a head of time and security became tighter. Most people might need to walk a long way from where they parked they're car if they don't live close enough.

Not only do you look at flowers, performances, food stands, and other entertainments are provided as well. In the year 2015, the Lilac Festival will run from May 8th to the 17th.
