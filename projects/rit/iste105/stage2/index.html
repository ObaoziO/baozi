Abstract:

No matter if you see Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) as an another field of many other studies, an outlook as a future career opportunity, or you might not even know what it is, you will come to learn more about HCI. In this website, a description of HCI's main components, such as its fields of studies, and its origin will be introduced. To those who are interested in HCI as a degree of college studies a list of schools (colleges/universities) offering HCI and/or related courses will be provided. Last but not least, money is always the main reason for one to go for a higher education, therefore a data on the HCI as a career will be included.
